Privacy Policy

This website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages.
When you access the site, Cloudflare processes technical data such as IP addresses to deliver the content and ensure security. This data is not stored or analyzed by the website operator.

Comments

When you submit a comment, the name you choose, the comment content, and the time of submission are stored and publicly displayed.

Local Storage

To allow you to edit or delete your own comments and to display replies to them, a technical token is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This token is used exclusively for this purpose and is not transmitted to third parties.

No Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics, tracking technologies, or third-party advertising services.
